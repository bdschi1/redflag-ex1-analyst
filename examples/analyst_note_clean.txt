ACME Corp (ACME) - Earnings Preview
Date: Q4 2024
Analyst: J. Smith

INVESTMENT THESIS
We maintain our OUTPERFORM rating on ACME Corp with a $45 price target based on
our DCF model using a 10% discount rate and 3% terminal growth assumption.

KEY POINTS
1. Revenue growth appears on track based on industry channel checks and public
   supplier commentary from recent earnings calls.

2. Management's guidance at the last investor day suggested 15-18% growth, which
   aligns with our independent analysis of public market data.

3. Competitive positioning remains strong according to third-party market share
   reports from Gartner and IDC.

RISKS
- Macroeconomic slowdown could impact enterprise spending
- New entrant competition in the SMB segment
- FX headwinds from EUR weakness

VALUATION
Our $45 target implies 22x forward earnings, in line with historical average and
peer group median. Sensitivity analysis suggests $40-$52 range based on +/- 2 turns
on the multiple.
